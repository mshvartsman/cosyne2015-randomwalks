Organizers

Amitai Shenhav is a C.V. Starr postdoctoral fellow at the Princeton Neuroscience Institute. He is interested in how our automatic biases interact with our careful weighing of potential outcomes when we make choices.

Michael Shvartsman is a post-doc at Princeton Neuroscience Institute. He is interested in the dynamics of decisions under changing context. He has also done some work in computational psycholinguistics, language emergence, and music cognition.

Robert Wilson is an Assistant Professor of Psychology and director of the Neuroscience of Reinforcement Learning Lab at the Univesity of Arizona. He is interested in the neuroscience of learning and decision making in humans.
