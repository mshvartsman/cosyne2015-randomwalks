Registration

The workshop will be held on Mar 9, 2015 at the Snowbird ski resort near Salt Lake City, UT. A free shuttle is available from the main conference to Snowbird at the end of the main Cosyne meeting. Shuttles can also be arranged directly from Salt Lake City Airport through Snowbird here.

Registration for the workshop is handled through the Cosyne 2015 website. The cost is $295 for the workshop only, or $495 including the main conference. Costs go up by $100 on Jan 31, so make sure to register early!

The workshop hotel is the Cliff Lodge at Snowbird, UT. You can reserve a room at the Snowbird site with group code URC315 for the discounted rate. There are no other hotels available nearby, though Salt Lake City is only about 40 minutes away by car. See the Cosyne 2015 website for details.
